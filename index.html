About the Author

My name is Mary Lumsden, was born in the state of the North Carolina, city of Greensboro. Resided there with my father and mother and six siblings, at the age of 13 years old. The family moved to Philadelphia, PA. We completed our education in Philadelphia. I continued business education and received certificates in Biblical learning and teaching education. I am grandmother, have two biological children, as son and a daughter. Other children through the marriage. Two daughters, total of five grands and two great grands. I enjoy arts and crafts, reading the bible, writing and attending church and traveling.
